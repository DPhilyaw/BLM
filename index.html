Allyship


It is very important for all white folx to remember that while the millstone of neoliberal capitalism is dragging us all down, we are NOT systematically oppressed in the same way that black folx & other POC are and have been in this country. In this moment and in any conversation about racial justice we are here to LISTEN FIRST, and to SUPPORT and to HELP.

Here are some resources on how to be an ally. Remember that it is NOT the responsibility of anyone oppressed to educate you, but it is ABSOLUTELY your responsibility to educate yourself and other privileged folx.

Ring theory is a simple method to visualize the correct way to support individuals (and communities) dealing with trauma and grief: LA Times (link)

Donating

Can’t go protest or help materially for whatever reason? Wondering where you can donate financially? A simple google for ‘(your city) mutual aid fund’ is the best place to start. National organizations have a lot more money and resources, and while they could always use more, please start by supporting local or smaller organizations. CHECK FIRST THAT THEY ARE ACCEPTING DONATIONS AND WATCH OUT FOR SCAMMERS.
